Privacy-Enhanced Federated Learning Framework

Our comprehensive Federated Learning (FL) System Framework is engineered to significantly augment the privacy, security, and operational resilience of machine learning models deployed in decentralized and distributed environments. The framework is composed of four interconnected core modules, collectively guaranteeing data integrity, defense against adversarial attacks, and authenticated inter-component communication.

Framework Overview and Architecture

Adversarial Defense and Resilience Module (ADRM)

  • Mitigation of Adversarial Attacks: Detects and mitigates model poisoning and Byzantine attacks.
  • Anomaly-Based Detection: Employs advanced statistical anomaly detection methods on shared gradients.
  • Validation of Updates: Enforces rigorous VALIDATION CHECKS on incoming model updates to ensure integrity.
  • Robustness Assurance: Maintains the operational ROBUSTNESS and reliability of the federated model.

Privacy Preservation Module (PPM)

  • Differential Privacy (DP) Integration: Applies CALIBRATED NOISE to model parameters to prevent data leakage from gradients.
  • Homomorphic Encryption (HE) Implementation: Utilizes HE for SECURE COMPUTATIONS over encrypted data, where feasible.
  • Proactive Auditing: Conducts regular, systematic PRIVACY AUDITS to verify compliance and effectiveness.
  • Data Obfuscation Guarantee: Ensures client-side data remains NON-IDENTIFIABLE throughout the training lifecycle.

Secure Communication Protocol Module (SCPM)

  • Secure Channel Establishment: Utilizes TLS 1.3 for END-TO-END ENCRYPTED and authenticated communication channels.
  • Message Integrity: Applies Hash-based Message Authentication Codes (HMAC) to guarantee DATA INTEGRITY during transit.
  • Mutual Authentication: Enforces MUTUAL CLIENT-SERVER VALIDATION using digital certificates.
