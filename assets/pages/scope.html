Framework Scope & Features

Our framework encompasses a modular design supporting key privacy and security features essential for modern Federated Learning applications.

Privacy Preservation

Techniques like differential privacy to protect data.

Attack Detection

Identifying and mitigating malicious client updates.

Secure Aggregation

Cryptographic methods to protect models during aggregation.

Encryption Protocols

End-to-end encryption for all communications.

Adaptive Learning

Models that adapt to new data and threats over time.

Modular Design

Easily adaptable architecture for various use-cases.
